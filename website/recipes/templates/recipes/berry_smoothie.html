Berry Smoothie

{% endblock %} {% block recipe_description %}

Description

A delicious smoothie made with frozen berries and acai!

I love fresh berries, especially blueberries, and this smoothie is an explosion of berry flavors.

This smoothie uses frozen fruit, as well as a fresh banana to make it smooth and creamy.

{% endblock %} {% block recipe_image %} {% endblock %} {% block recipe_tips %}

Tips

Feel free to subsitute your favorite frozen berries into this recipe.

It can be tough to get the correct consistency in the blender, but add apple juice if the mixture is too thick and isn't mixing correctly.

{% endblock %} {% block recipe_ingredients %}

List of Ingredients

  • 1 Packet of Frozen Unsweetened Acai
  • 1 Cup of Frozen Blueberries
  • 1 Cup of Frozen Strawberries
  • 1/3 Cup of Frozen Dark Cherries
  • 1 Fresh Banana
  • 1 Cup of Water
  • 1/2 Cup of Apple Juice
{% endblock %} {% block recipe_steps %}

Recipe Steps

  1. Using a blender, start by adding the water and then all of the frozen fruit
  2. Add the banana to the blender by breaking it up into pieces (3 or 4)
  3. Blend on a medium speed until the mixture is smooth and everything is incorporated together
  4. Add the apple juice to make the consistency more liquid
  5. Pour the mixture into a cup and enjoy!
