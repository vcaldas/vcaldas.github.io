Jasmine Rice

{% endblock %} {% block recipe_description %}

Description

Jasmine rice is a staple and I think it has a better flavor than regular rice.

Once I learned how to make this dish, it comes out perfectly every time!

{% endblock %} {% block recipe_image %} {% endblock %} {% block recipe_tips %}

Tips

Use a pot with a lid that sits tightly on the pot to prevent excess steam from escaping.

After the rice is done cooking, fluff the rice a bit with a wooden spoon but then re-cover the rice in the pot to continue the cooking process for 5-10 more minutes.

{% endblock %} {% block recipe_ingredients %}

List of Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Jasmine Rice
  • 4 Cups of Cold Water
{% endblock %} {% block recipe_steps %}

Recipe Steps

  1. Pour the rice and the cold water into a medium-sized pot
  2. With the pot uncovered, heat on medium heat until the water/rice is slightly boiling
  3. Mix with a wooden spoon just a few times
  4. Cover the pot and reduce the heat to as low as possible
  5. Cook for 17 minutes
  6. Once done, fluff the rice a bit with a wooden spoon but then re-cover the rice in the pot to continue the cooking process for 5-10 more minutes
  7. Serve directly from the pot or put on a plate to serve family-style (the rice stays hot for a surprisingly long time on a plate!)
